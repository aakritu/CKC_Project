General Questions

What does a class workflow look like?

+

Our classes follow a structured yet flexible workflow that includes live sessions, interactive assignments, group discussions, and personal mentoring. Students can access course materials 24/7 and learn at their own pace.

How do online classes work?

+

Online classes are conducted through our state-of-the-art learning platform. Students attend live virtual sessions, participate in discussions, submit assignments, and receive immediate feedback from instructors.

What support is available for students?

+

We provide comprehensive support including 24/7 technical assistance, academic counseling, career guidance, and access to our extensive learning resources library.
